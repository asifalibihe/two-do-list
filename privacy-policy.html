Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 18, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Two-Do List. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When creating an account, we collect your name, email address, and optionally a profile picture.
  • Task Data: Tasks, reminders, and lists you add to the app.
  • Chat Data: Messages you exchange with your partner through the app's chat feature.
  • Profile Information: Your uploaded profile picture, which is visible to your shared partner.
  • Usage Data: We collect app usage data, interactions, and device information to improve performance.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's functionality.
  • To enable task sharing, real-time updates, and reminders.
  • To facilitate the chat feature between shared users.
  • To display your profile picture within the app for personalized use.
  • To analyze app usage and improve the user experience.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. Your information is only shared with the partner you invite to use the app for shared tasks, chats, and lists.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to secure your data. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Choices

You can:

  • Edit or delete your tasks, lists, and messages at any time.
  • Update or remove your profile picture in the app.
  • Manage notification preferences within the app settings.

7. Chat Feature

Our app includes a chat feature that allows you to exchange messages with your partner. Messages are private and visible only to the two connected users. We do not access or share your chat data unless required by law.

8. Profile Information

When you upload a profile picture, it will be visible to your shared partner within the app. You can update or delete your profile picture at any time.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You will be notified of any changes through the app or via email.
